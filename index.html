WELCOME TO UW VSA

A message from our acting President

Welcome to the University of Waterloo Vietnamese Students’ Association!
Just like you, I started off as a regular member. But when joined UWVSA, I knew that I wanted to achieve so much more with my involvement in the club. Given the opportunity to excel, I reached to becoming the President in fall term 2014. Since then, I’ve grown into the leader that I am today with the support of my executive team aka my second family. I see the club more than just a student-run organization, but a club that is filled with students who come together for one resolution (not ‘Pho’). That resolution being, to build relationships. UWVSA helps you form relationships with peers who share your interest, gives you an opportunity to become a leader, making contributions as a club member, and making a lasting impact on Waterloo campus. It is a great way to translate what you have learned in the classroom into reality but essentially it is an outlet from the stress of academics. It has been a rewarding experience as I watch the club and its members grow, and to be able to shape UWVSA with my vision moving forward. Whatever your passion or interest, it's bound to be represented in some form by a club. Your involvement with UWVSA is what you make of it!

- Nellie Nguyen
