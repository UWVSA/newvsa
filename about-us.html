UWVSA is a registered student organization at the University of Waterloo that aims to promote understanding of the Vietnamese culture and providing a supportive community for its members. We welcome individuals from all backgrounds to join us in our mission to celebrate and promote cultural diversity at the University of Waterloo and its surrounding communities. The UWVSA organizes social, cultural and charitable events within the group and in partnership with the local community, as well as provide opportunities for student leadership development within the UWVSA student body. We encourage you to participate in our events to learn more about UWVSA!
