Tammy Tran

Events Coordinator

2A Honours English: Rhetoric & Professional Writing

Hi, My name’s Tammy! I’m originally from Barrie, and growing up, I was very immersed in Vietnamese culture. When I moved to Waterloo for school, I joined VSA because I wanted to stay connected with my roots and to meet other students who also shared a similar appreciation for Vietnamese culture. This year I am proud to be a part of the Executive Team for VSA, and I’m looking forward to the exciting events that we have planned to come. When I don’t have my head buried in a book (typical English major!) I enjoy baking, DIY crafts (I spend way too much time on Pinterest) and in the winter, I love to hit up the slopes and go skiing. Please feel free to stay hi or send me a message if you share any interests, I’m always happy to make new friends!
