VSA Executive Profile

Mitchell Nguyen

Events Coordinator

Honours Liberal Arts

I grew up in a very non-Asian town, Alliston. My parents were heavy with Asian culture, they made sure I knew where I was coming from and that I would know about my culture. I got interested in VSA through my Vietnamese friends during orientation who had joined VSA that very same term. I was surprised when there was a VSA and hoped to join in my first Winter Term, but due to the hectic schedule of that year’s exec team they were unable to run that Winter Term. My hobbies comprise of reading novels, sports, video games and dancing. I am an active member in the Waterloo Community being the Vice-President of the University of Waterloo Breakers and a member of University of Waterloo Hip-hop community.
