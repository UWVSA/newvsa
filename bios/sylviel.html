VSA Executive Profile

Sylvie Le

Marketing Executive

Global Business and Digital Arts

Bonjour a tous! Je m'appelle Sylvie! That's right, I can speak Frenglemese (a.k.a French, English, and Vietnamese). I was raised in an all European school system in Montreal. I was never able to meet more than four Vietnamese people for 15 years of my life. After moving to Ontario and attending University, never in my life have I seen this many Orientals gathered in one area like uWaterloo. Seeing this chance to learn more about my own culture and people, I reached out to VSA where I met and made mesmerizing memories and friendships. VSA showed me so much love, that I had to be part of the exec team in hopes that I can pass down the same experience to future VSA members.
