VSA Executive Profile

Nellie Nguyen

President

Social Development Studies

Hello lovelies! My name is Nellie (yes like the it’s getting hot in here rapper). I’m your typical small town girl who aside from my family was never really exposed to Vietnamese culture. As I entered my first year at the University of Waterloo, joining UWVSA was a must! No questions asked. UWVSA became my support and home. Being an active member and executive first year led me to become VSA’s President second year (yes, call me crazy, so young)! Aside from UWVSA, I enjoy meeting new people, making fried bananas, and I’m attempting to teach myself how to play the ukulele! I aim to change the world one smile at a time.
